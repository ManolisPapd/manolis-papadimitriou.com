Privacy Policy

Personal Information

  • Extensions are not accessing nor using personal data such as accounts, passwords or personal information.
  • Personal data of the website that the extensions are active, are not stored or distributed.
  • Personal information with technical aspect (IP address, user's OS, Chrome settings) are not accessed, stored or distributed.

Extensions Usage

  • Client side viewing is manipulated (DOM manipulation).
  • Tracking of the current page is implemented without storing any activity.
  • Multiple calls to the website that the extensions will be active are prevented.
  • Pop-Up window on the extension menu bar, will contain URLs that will redirect to safe and recognized platforms such as Chrome Web Store and GitHub.
  • Extensions are not blocked by and will not block the functionality of other extensions.
